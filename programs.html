Fight Club Disciplines

Built for underground competition, rough sparring, and real fight readiness.

01 / Underground MMA

All-in combat integration. Hard sparring loops designed to pressure-test your endurance, setups, and transition fluidity under extreme stress.

02 / Rough Grappling

Submission work built for the floor. Focuses on absolute control, heavy positioning, and rapid transitions to lock out fights quickly.

03 / Striking Mechanics

Precision power generation. Master high-impact punches, structural elbow tracking, and kinetic combat movement to break guards cleanly.

04 / Defense Tactics

Uncompromising evasions, shell blocking, and framing strategies. Minimize incoming damage, counter aggressively, and control the pace.

05 / Kick Assault

Explosive footwork and high-impact kicking. Kill distance, punish openings, and keep the opponent off balance.

06 / Dirty Judo

Throws and trips from the clinch. Pressure-driven control to collapse the opponent and keep aggression locked in.

07 / Chaos Conditioning

High-intensity circuits to build fight endurance, sharp recovery, and the will to push after being broken down.

08 / Mental Resilience

Psychological training for aggression control, fear management, and the mindset required to stay calm under fire.

09 / Club Strategy

Team drills, opponent study, and tactical planning that turns raw energy into effective fight execution.
